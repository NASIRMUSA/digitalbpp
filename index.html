FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and packages

How long does CAC registration take?

CAC registration typically takes 5-10 business days from the time we receive all required documents. We handle the entire process including name reservation, documentation, and certificate collection. You'll receive regular updates throughout the process.

What's included in the Full Branding Package?

Our Full Branding Package includes custom logo design (with multiple concepts and revisions), brand color palette, typography selection, brand style guide, business card design, letterhead design, and all source files. You'll own complete rights to all designs.

How does the payment process work?

We accept payment via bank transfer, card payment, or installment plans. For most packages, we require 50% upfront payment to begin work, with the balance due upon completion. Premium clients can discuss flexible payment terms with our team.

Can I customize a package to fit my needs?

Absolutely! While we offer three standard packages, we understand that every business is unique. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements, and we'll create a custom package tailored to your needs and budget.

What documents do I need for CAC registration?

You'll need: valid ID cards of directors/shareholders, passport photographs, proposed company names (at least 3 options), company address, and details of directors/shareholders. We'll provide a complete checklist and guide you through the documentation process.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! All packages include post-completion support. Premium Package clients get priority support for 6 months. We're here to help with any questions, technical issues, or additional services you may need as your business grows.
